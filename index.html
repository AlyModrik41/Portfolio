CLASS: COMPUTER_VISION

Football Analysis System

An end-to-end CV pipeline that watches broadcast football footage and turns it into tactical data — who ran where, how fast, and who had the ball.

  • Dual YOLOv8x models track 22 players, 1 referee, and the ball across 1,750+ frames at 87%+ confidence.
  • A custom position-based ID remapper collapsed 800+ noisy track IDs down to 28 stable ones on top of ByteTrack.
  • Lucas-Kanade optical flow cancels camera motion; a perspective transform converts pixel space to real meters for accurate speed in km/h.
  • KMeans kit-color clustering auto-assigns teams and exports per-player distance, speed, touches, and possession to CSV.
